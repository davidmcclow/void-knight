# VOID KNIGHT v8.1 - ASPECT OF THE VOID (BASE CLASS)

*"The void answers to those who master it—whether through study, meditation, or force of will. I have chosen my path, and through it, I command the stillness between moments, the silence that devours magic itself."*

---


## CLASS OVERVIEW

**Hit Die:** d8  
**Base Attack Bonus:** Full  
**Good Saves:** Fortitude, Will  
**Skill Ranks per Level:** 6 + Intelligence modifier  

**Class Skills:** Acrobatics (Dex), Climb (Str), Heal (Wis), Intimidate (Cha), Knowledge (arcana) (Int), Knowledge (planes) (Int), Perception (Wis), Sense Motive (Wis), Spellcraft (Int), Stealth (Dex), Survival (Wis)

**Weapon and Armor Proficiency:** Void Knights are proficient with all simple and martial weapons, light armor, medium armor, and shields (except tower shields).

## CLASS FEATURES

### **Void Aspect (Extraordinary)** 
At 1st level, you must choose **Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma** as your **void aspect**. This choice represents your philosophical and practical approach to mastering void energy and **cannot be changed** once selected.

**Intelligence (The Void Scholar):** You approach the void through study and analysis, comprehending nothingness through systematic observation and cataloging of absence. You understand the void as a measurable phenomenon that can be predicted and controlled through knowledge.

**Wisdom (The Void Mystic):** You approach the void through meditation and spiritual enlightenment, achieving harmony with emptiness itself. You perceive the void intuitively, becoming one with the silence between thoughts and the stillness between moments.

**Charisma (The Void Channeler):** You approach the void through force of personality and strength of will, commanding nothingness to bend to your determination. The void responds to your presence, your conviction imposing order upon absence itself.

**Mechanical Effects:**
The chosen ability score determines:
- The size of your void pool (class level + chosen ability modifier)
- The save DCs for all your void knight abilities (10 + ½ class level + chosen ability modifier)
- Bonuses granted by certain class features (as specified in their descriptions)

This choice does **not** alter your class skills, base attack bonus, or saving throw progressions.

*Three paths, one destination. Whether you think, feel, or commandâ€”the void answers.*

---

### **Void Pool (Supernatural)**
At 1st level, you gain a pool of Void Points (VP) equal to your class level + your **void aspect modifier**. This pool replenishes after 8 hours of rest or meditation.

You spend VP to activate your void abilities. Your save DC for void abilities is **10 + ½ your class level + your void aspect modifier**.

*Whether through scholarly discipline, meditative clarity, or commanding will, you have learned to harness the void's power.*

---

### **Void Strike (Supernatural)**
At 1st level, as a swift action, you can spend 1 VP to channel void energy into your weapons for 1 minute. While Void Strike is active, all your melee and ranged weapon attacks deal additional force damage. Targets hit must make a Fortitude save (DC 10 + ½ your class level + your void aspect modifier) or take full Void Strike damage. On a successful save, they take half damage.

- **1st-4th level:** +1d6 force damage
- **5th-8th level:** +2d6 force damage
- **9th-12th level:** +3d6 force damage
- **13th-16th level:** +4d6 force damage
- **17th-20th level:** +5d6 force damage

This force damage bypasses all damage reduction.

**At 10th level:** Duration increases to 10 minutes.  
**At 15th level:** Activating Void Strike becomes a free action.  
**At 20th level (Void Perfection):** Void Strike becomes permanent and no longer costs VP.

*Your weapons become extensions of the void itself, striking with the force of pure negation.*

---

---

### **Reactive Counterspell (Supernatural)**
At 2nd level, you can counter spells with pure void energy as an immediate action. When you see a creature within **medium range (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)** casting a spell, you can spend **1 VP** to attempt to counter it.

**Optional Identification:** As a free action before deciding to counter, you may attempt a Spellcraft check (DC 15 + spell level) to identify the exact spell being cast. This does not prevent you from using Reactive Counterspell if you fail or choose not to attempt the check.

Make a **dispel check (1d20 + your class level)** against **DC 11 + the spell's caster level**. If successful, the spell is countered.

**Boosting:** You can spend additional VP (up to your **void aspect modifier**) to gain a +2 bonus per additional VP spent on your dispel check.

**At 9th level (Greater Reactive Counterspell):** You can use Reactive Counterspell **twice per round** (but still only once per triggering spell).

*You sense the gathering magic and reach out with practiced ease, smoothing the disturbance before it manifests.*

---

### **Void Dispel (Spell-Like Ability)**
At 2nd level, you can spend **1 VP** to use *dispel magic* as a standard action (CL = your class level). At 9th level, this functions as *greater dispel magic*.

**At 15th level:** You can use Void Dispel as a move action.

---

### **Intuitive Spellcraft (Extraordinary)**
At 3rd level, you can use your **void aspect modifier** instead of your Intelligence modifier for all Spellcraft checks. This represents your inherent understanding of magic through your chosen path—whether analytical knowledge, spiritual awareness, or commanding presence.

*You don't just study magic—you perceive its patterns through your connection to the void.*

---

### **Null Zone (Supernatural)**
At 3rd level, you can spend **1 VP** as a **swift action** to activate an aura of void energy that disrupts magic around you. This aura lasts for **1 minute** and moves with you.

All creatures within the radius (including you) gain a bonus on saving throws against spells and spell-like abilities. Additionally, enemy spellcasters must make a concentration check (DC = 15 + spell level) to cast spells within the Null Zone.

- **3rd-4th level:** +2 bonus, 10 ft. radius
- **5th-9th level:** +4 bonus, 20 ft. radius
- **10th-14th level:** +6 bonus, 30 ft. radius
- **15th-19th level:** +8 bonus, 40 ft. radius
- **20th level:** +10 bonus, 50 ft. radius

**At 10th level:** Duration increases to 10 minutes.  
**At 20th level (Void Perfection):** Null Zone becomes permanent and no longer costs VP.

*Your presence creates a zone of stability where magic is harder to maintain.*

---

### **Void Step (Supernatural)**
Starting at 3rd level, as a **move action**, you can spend 1 VP to teleport up to 30 feet to any location you can see. This movement does not provoke attacks of opportunity.

**At 9th level:** Void Step becomes a swift action.  
**At 15th level:** Range increases to 60 feet.  
**At 18th level:** Void Step becomes an immediate action (usable even when it's not your turn).

*You slip through the cracks in reality, traveling the paths between spaces.*

---

### **Void Regeneration (Extraordinary)**
Starting at 4th level, whenever you successfully counter or dispel a spell with Reactive Counterspell or Void Dispel, you regain VP based on the spell's level. This ability can only restore VP **once per round**, regardless of how many spells you counter or dispel in that round.

**VP Regained:**
- **1st-3rd level spells:** Regain 1 VP
- **4th-5th level spells:** Regain 2 VP
- **6th-7th level spells:** Regain 3 VP
- **8th-9th level spells:** Regain 4 VP

**Note:** You can gain the Improved Void Regeneration feat to allow this ability to trigger a second time per round.

*Successfully negating magic reinforces your connection to the void, restoring your inner stillness.*

---

### **Void Sense (Supernatural)**
Starting at 5th level, you gain constant *detect magic* out to 60 feet. Additionally, you gain a **+4 bonus on Perception checks** to notice invisible creatures, magical traps, magical auras, or other magically concealed phenomena.

**Spell Level Awareness:** When you see a creature casting a spell within 60 feet, you instinctively sense the spell's level (cantrip, 1st, 2nd, etc.) as a free action. You do not learn the spell's identity unless you succeed on a Spellcraft check, but you know its approximate power. This allows you to make informed decisions when using Reactive Counterspell.

**At 17th level (Void Clarity):** This becomes permanent *true seeing* and you gain permanent *mind blank*, making you immune to divination spells, mental detection, and mind-affecting effects.

*The void reveals all that magic would hide, and you sense the intensity of gathering power even before it manifests.*

---

### **Disruptive (Bonus Feat)** 
At 6th level, you gain **Disruptive** as a bonus feat. You do not need to meet the normal prerequisites.

**Disruptive:** Your training in disrupting magic makes you a threat to spellcasters. Enemies provoke attacks of opportunity from you when they cast spells. The Spellcraft DC to cast defensively increases by +4 for enemies within your threatened area.

*Your very presence destabilizes the concentration of nearby spellcasters.*

---

### **Efficient Dispelling (Extraordinary)**
Starting at 7th level, you gain a **+2 competence bonus** on dispel checks (including Void Dispel, Reactive Counterspell, and Greater Void Strike).

*Your understanding of magical patterns allows you to unravel them with greater precision.*

---

### **Void Aspect Mastery I (Extraordinary)**
Starting at 7th level, your deepening connection to your chosen aspect grants unique benefits.

**Intelligence (Analytical Insight):**
- You gain a +2 bonus on all Knowledge checks and Spellcraft checks
- Once per day, you may reroll a failed dispel check (including Void Dispel, Reactive Counterspell, and Greater Void Strike)

**Wisdom (Centered Awareness):**
- You gain a +2 bonus to Will saves
- You gain a +2 bonus to Perception and Sense Motive checks

**Charisma (Forceful Presence):**
- You gain a +2 bonus on all Charisma-based skill checks
- Once per day as a swift action, you may grant one ally within 30 feet a bonus equal to your void aspect modifier on their next attack roll, saving throw, or skill check (their choice)

*Your aspect becomes an integral part of your combat style and capabilities.*

---

### **Siphon Magic (Supernatural)**
Starting at 8th level, whenever you successfully counter a spell with Reactive Counterspell, you regain hit points equal to the spell's level.

*You don't just negate magic, you consume it, feeding on the energy of broken spells.*

---

### **Greater Void Dispel (Spell-Like Ability)**
At 9th level, your Void Dispel ability functions as *greater dispel magic* instead of *dispel magic*.

*Your mastery over dispelling magic deepens, allowing you to unravel even the most complex enchantments.*

---

### **Spell Resistance (Extraordinary)**
Starting at 10th level, you gain spell resistance equal to 11 + your class level. You can lower this SR as a standard action and raise it again as a free action.

**At 19th level:** Your SR increases to 16 + your class level.

*Magic slides off you like water off stone.*

---

### **Spellbreaker (Bonus Feat)**
At 11th level, you gain **Spellbreaker** as a bonus feat. You do not need to meet the normal prerequisites.

**Spellbreaker:** When an enemy fails to cast defensively within your threatened area, they provoke an attack of opportunity from you.

*Your strikes don't just wound the body—they punish failed spellcasting attempts.*

---

### **Efficient Saves (Extraordinary)**
Starting at 12th level, you gain a **+2 bonus on all saving throws** against spells and spell-like abilities. This bonus **stacks with the bonus provided by Null Zone**.

*Your centered awareness makes magical effects slide past you like water off stone.*

---

### **Void Aspect Mastery II (Extraordinary)**
Starting at 12th level, your mastery over your aspect reaches new heights.

**Intelligence (Prepared Mind):**
- You may take 10 on all Knowledge and Spellcraft checks, even when threatened or distracted
- Add your Intelligence modifier to initiative checks

**Wisdom (Void Meditation):**
- Once per day, you may meditate for 10 minutes to regain Wisdom modifier in VP
- Add your Wisdom modifier to initiative checks

**Charisma (Rallying Presence):**
- All allies within 30 feet gain a bonus equal to ½ your Charisma modifier (minimum +1) on saving throws against spells and spell-like abilities
- This is an aura effect that moves with you and is always active

*Your aspect has become inseparable from your identity as a void knight.*

---

### **Greater Void Strike (Supernatural)**
Starting at 13th level, whenever you hit a creature while Void Strike is active, you automatically attempt to dispel all ongoing magical effects of **3rd level or lower** on that creature. Make a separate dispel check for each effect (1d20 + your class level + 2 [from Efficient Dispelling] vs. DC 11 + caster level). This does not cost VP or an actionâ€”it happens automatically on hit.

You can only use Greater Void Strike once per round.

*Your strikes pierce not just armor, but the very fabric of magical defenses.*

---

### **Void Recovery (Supernatural)**
Starting at 14th level, whenever you regain VP through Void Regeneration, you regain **1 additional VP** (so you regain ½ spell level + 1 VP, minimum 2 total).

**Examples at 14th level:**
- 1st-2nd level spell: Regain 1 + 1 = **2 VP**
- 5th-6th level spell: Regain 3 + 1 = **4 VP**
- 9th level spell: Regain 4 + 1 = **5 VP**

*Your mastery over void energy allows you to draw even more power from disrupted magic.*

---

### **Supreme Null Zone (Supernatural)**
At 15th level, once per day, you can spend 5 VP when activating Null Zone to transform it into a limited antimagic field for 1 minute. While Supreme Null Zone is active:

- **Antimagic Effect:** Functions as *antimagic field* (suppresses all magical effects within the radius)
- **Void Knight Exception:** Your void knight class abilities (Void Strike, Void Step, Reactive Counterspell, etc.) are **NOT suppressed** within this field
- **Radius:** Same as your current Null Zone radius (40 ft at 15th, 50 ft at 20th)

**At 20th level (Void Perfection):** You can use Supreme Null Zone twice per day, and it costs 0 VP to activate.

*You don't just resist magicâ€”you erase it from existence itself, creating a zone of pure void.*

---

### **Void Clarity (Supernatural)**
At 17th level, your Void Sense evolves into perfect perception. You gain permanent *true seeing* and permanent *mind blank*, making you immune to divination spells, mental detection, illusions, invisibility, and mind-affecting effects.

*The void reveals all truth, and nothing can cloud your awareness.*

---

### **Spell Redirection (Supernatural)**
Starting at 19th level, when you successfully counter a spell with Reactive Counterspell, you can spend 1 additional VP to redirect that spell to a new target of your choice within the spell's range. Use your void aspect modifier to determine variable effects.

*You don't just stop their spellâ€”you turn it against them.*

---

### **Void Perfection (Supernatural)**
At 20th level, you achieve perfect mastery over the void. You gain the following benefits:

- Void Strike becomes permanent and costs 0 VP
- Null Zone becomes permanent and costs 0 VP
- Supreme Null Zone can be used twice per day and costs 0 VP
- Your type changes to Outsider (native)
- You gain darkvision 60 feet (or extend existing darkvision by 60 feet)
- You no longer need to eat, drink, or breathe (but must still sleep to regain VP)

*You have become one with the voidâ€”a living embodiment of anti-magic.*

---

### **Void Immunity (Extraordinary)**
At 20th level, choose one school of magic (abjuration, conjuration, divination, enchantment, evocation, illusion, necromancy, or transmutation). You become completely immune to all spells and spell-like abilities from that school.

Once chosen, this cannot be changed.

*You have achieved perfect understanding of one magical school's patternsâ€”you exist in a state of permanent negation against it.*
