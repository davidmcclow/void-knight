# Void Knight

*A martial anti-caster homebrew class for Pathfinder 1e, with a full conversion to D&D 5e.*

The Void Knight is an aggressive spellbreaker — a warrior who doesn't sling spells, but devours them. Powered by a **Void Pool (VP)** resource system, the class counters, dispels, and unravels enemy magic at melee range, projecting a **Null Zone** that suppresses casters and rewards fearless positioning. Where most anti-magic options in d20 systems are passive or situational, the Void Knight makes hunting spellcasters an active, resource-driven combat loop: dispel to fuel your pool, spend your pool to break their magic.

### Archetypes (Pathfinder 1e)

| Archetype | Role | Identity |
|---|---|---|
| **Void Reaver** | Offense | Spell-eater; burst damage and Void Frenzy |
| **Null Guardian** | Defense | Tank; Guardian's Intercept, Sheltering Void, Protective Siphon |
| **Null Mender** | Support | Healer; Sanctuary of Renewal turns the Null Zone into a regeneration field |

The 5e conversion adds three more specializations: **Void Hazard Diver** (utility bodyguard), **Void Interrogator** (Charisma-based face), and **Monk of the Void** (Wisdom-based unarmed striker).

## Design Notes

This class has gone through multiple balance passes based on playtest feedback. Selected decisions and their rationale:

- **Eliminated dead levels (v3.0)** — every level from 1–20 grants a feature; unified VP scaling replaced ad hoc bonuses.
- **Void Dispel costs 1 VP per use (v4.1)** — free at-will dispelling trivialized caster encounters; a VP cost forces tactical resource decisions.
- **First specialization moved from 8th to 7th level (v4.1)** — removed the last remaining dead level and smoothed the mid-game power curve.
- **Removed Devouring Counterspell from Void Reaver (v4.1)** — the combined counterspell + spell-theft loop outpaced other archetypes' action economy.
- **Reworked Null Mender's 6th level (v4.1)** — replaced Restoration Strike and Mender's Armor with **Sanctuary of Renewal**: allies inside the Null Zone gain Fast Healing equal to half the Mender's level. Simpler to run at the table, and the sustain math (Fast Healing + Restorative Aura) was modeled explicitly before shipping.
- **5e conversion (v5.0)** — swift/immediate actions mapped to bonus actions/reactions, dispel checks became Intelligence checks (DC 10 + spell level), and the Spellbreaker VP-recovery loop was preserved as the class's core identity.

## Balance Philosophy

- **Tier 1 (1–4):** Basic counterspelling; a small VP pool makes every point matter.
- **Tier 2 (5–10):** The full anti-caster loop comes online (Extra Attack + Spellbreaker).
- **Tier 3 (11–16):** Mobility and action-economy upgrades (improved Void Step, bonus-action dispels).
- **Tier 4 (17–20):** Capstone mastery of anti-magic without breaking the game.

Each specialization trades away part of the base kit for focused power, keeping the base class the baseline rather than the floor.
